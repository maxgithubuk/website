The Max Moser Homepage

I am a mixed-methods social science researcher. My interests include psychotherapy research, public health, market research, philosophy of mind, and critical and phenomenological psychiatry. I am also very interested in the history of psychiatry, history in general, sacred music, country music, and Elvis. I also really like movies. Although I basically love all movies, off the top of my head I count Billy Wilder, Howard Hawks, Preston Sturges, Chantal Ackerman, Ingmar Bergman, and Federico Fellini as some of my favourite filmmakers. If you would like to get in touch with me, please e-mail me at max[dot]moser@ucl.ac.uk.
